I offer a full range of UI/UX design services including user research, wireframing, prototyping, visual design, and usability testing for web and mobile applications.

I primarily use Figma, Adobe Photoshop, and Sketch for design. For prototyping, I use Figma's built-in prototyping tools along with InVision and Framer.

I primarily focus on design, but I can collaborate closely with developers to ensure the final product matches the design specifications perfectly.

Project timelines vary based on complexity. A simple landing page may take 1–2 weeks, while a full app design can take 4–8 weeks including research, iteration, and final delivery.
